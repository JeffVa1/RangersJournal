It started with blood and it hasn’t stopped since. They thought we were just running, just surviving the God King’s reach like everyone else trying not to be crushed beneath it. But somewhere between a lost  scroll, burning cities, and the dead refusing to stay buried, it became something else. Something personal. Now they carry secrets that feel heavier than steel, cures that can save a city choking on plague, relics that shouldn’t exist, and truths that mark us for death. They've seen dragons blot out the sun, watched men break under whispers only they could hear, and held fathers as they died in chains beneath a world already rotting. And still… they keep moving.

Because something is changing. The land twists, magic fractures, and the God King’s grip tightens through masks, whispers, and things far worse than soldiers. They've walked through cursed towns where the dead call you by name, fought horrors clawing their way up from beneath the earth, and uncovered fragments of a past the world was meant to forget. Now there are hundreds who look to them, not as survivors, but as something dangerous: hope. But hope in this world doesn’t shine, it burns. And the closer they get to the truth, to Irillis, to whatever is awakening beneath it all… the more it feels like they were never meant to win. Only to resist long enough for something worse to notice them.
